Frequently asked questions

What you need
to know

How much does it cost?+
In the case of a sale, we are paid from the delivered result. Our fee is therefore directly dependent on the success of the transaction and the price achieved.
How is a company's value determined?+
A company's value is determined using a combination of methods, including financial statement analysis, market comparison, discounted cash flows and assessment of intangible assets. We also consider growth potential and industry specifics.
What does the sales process look like?+
A sale typically involves preparing the company, identifying potential buyers, negotiations, due diligence and closing the transaction. The process can take several months to a year, depending on the complexity.
How can advisory help with a sale?+
Our advisory helps you maximize transaction value, identify ideal buyers, prepare the company for sale, negotiate the best terms and efficiently manage the entire process. Our experience and market knowledge save you time and minimize risks.
How to finance a company acquisition?+
Acquisition financing can include a combination of own resources, bank loans, private equity investments or seller financing. We help you identify the most suitable financing structure and introduce you to potential capital sources.
